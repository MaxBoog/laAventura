Guatapé Tour

Escape the dynamic city and join us to the beautiful Guatapé. A unique traditional town next to stunning lakes and a mysterious rock that towers into the sky. We’ll show you a marvelous view, provide you a very good lunch and show you the colors of the town. Besides we offer many options like taking a boat across the lakes, racing on jet skies, cycle through the mountains and ride horses through the town.

Includes: Transport, Tour guide, Stone tickets, Lunch

Duration: 9H Price: 1P 220$ , 2P 120$ p.p. 3P 90$ p.p., 4P 70$ p.p.

Schedule: pick-up > Stone climb (piedra del peñol > lunch (traditional or vegetarian), tour through the colourful town (guatape) > drop-off hotel

Optional:

  1. Speed boat: 150$ an hour.
  2. Party boat: 150$ an hour.
  3. Luxurious Party boat: 180$ an hour.

Jungle Stay

An EXCLUSIVE mde all-in opportunity. Discover an area, untouched by tourism, displaying beautiful nature. The jungle surrounding the Magdalena river was unsafe for many years. But now, since peace has come, the area is open for travellers. Enjoy beautiful rides, monkeys & other wildlife, fishing, swimming, marvelous waterfalls and much more.

Includes: Bus transport tickets, river canoe transport, eco hotel room, fresh meals, a guide by the creeks, cozy wood fires, fishing gear.

Duration: 3 Day 2 Nights, 7 hour trip with bus and boat.

Price: 1 person $360, 2 persons $478, 3 persons $572, 4 person $688

Schedule: pick-up > Stone climb (piedra del peñol > lunch (traditional or vegetarian), tour through the colourful town (guatape) > drop-off hotel

Optional: Full-day fishing trip by the river on a motorized boat with driver. 120$.
